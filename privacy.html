Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Rebel Dev is a trading name of Rebel AI Ltd (company number SC839626). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.

What we collect

When you fill in our contact form, we collect: your name, company name (optional), email address, project type, and message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and discuss potential work together.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide to: respond to your enquiry within one working day, discuss your project requirements, and send you a quote or scope if appropriate. We do not use your data for marketing unless you specifically ask us to keep you updated.

Who sees your data

Your data is only accessible to the Rebel Dev team and is used solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Form submissions are processed through Formspree, a secure form handling service. You can read their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

How we store your data

Form submissions are transmitted securely via HTTPS and processed by Formspree. We do not store your data in any other database. Email correspondence is handled through Microsoft 365 with standard encryption.

Cookies

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. This may place cookies on your device. No personal data is shared beyond what is necessary for ad measurement. You can opt out of Google tracking at ads.google.com/settings.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to: request a copy of any data we hold about you, ask us to delete your data, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email contact@rebeldev.co.uk.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at contact@rebeldev.co.uk.

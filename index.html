Case Study  /  UK Logistics Group

A pricing tool built around how they actually sell.

A UK logistics group needed a better way to price. Three staff members were spending hours each week manually cross-referencing rate cards across four different operators before every quote.

We sat with their sales team, learned how they actually quoted, and built the tool around that workflow. It pulls every rate card into one place and gives them instant answers.

The spreadsheets are gone. The team quotes in minutes instead of hours, with fewer errors.
